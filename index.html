About us

Maani Solar is a MENA based company that specializes in the delivery of turn-key PV solar renewable energy solutions using revered German technologies.

This includes the complete engineering, procurement, installation, testing, commissioning, operation & maintenance of photovoltaic solar systems that are fit to offset almost any electrical demand.
